Common Questions

How long does the workshop take?

90 minutes total: 10-min intro/demo, 60 min of hands-on creation, 20 min for group photos and wrap-up. We can adjust timing if you need shorter or longer sessions.

What phone models do you support?

All current iPhone and Samsung models, plus Google Pixel, OnePlus, and most others. We collect phone models when you book and bring the right cases for everyone.

Do people need to be artistic or "crafty"?

Absolutely not. This isn't about art skills. It's about the experience. The "I'm not creative" people often end up having the most fun and making the best cases.

What do we need to provide?

Just a flat, clean workspace like a conference room, open area, or breakroom with tables. We bring all supplies, materials, tools, and handle setup and cleanup.

Can we host this at a restaurant, bar, or offsite venue?

Yes! As long as there's table space and it's okay with the venue. We've done workshops at coworking spaces, breweries, and private event spaces.

What if someone doesn't want to participate?

No pressure. Some folks prefer to watch and socialize. But in our experience, curiosity wins and almost everyone joins in once they see it happening.

How far in advance should we book?

Ideally 2-3 weeks to ensure we have the right supplies and cases. But if you're in a pinch, reach out. We'll do our best to accommodate rush requests.

Do you offer private/bachelorette party packages?

Yes! We do bachelorette parties, birthdays, and girls' nights. Pricing starts at $500 for smaller groups. Let's chat about what you're envisioning.
